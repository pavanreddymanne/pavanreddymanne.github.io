About me

I am currently working as a Lead Engineer at WiSig Networks, where I am involved in algorithm design and development of 5G systems. I received my Ph.D. and M.Tech degrees from Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad under the guidance of Prof. Kiran Kuchi.

Research Areas

  • Wireless communications.

  • Massive MIMO and beamforming.

  • Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces

  • Scheduler designs for cellular technoligies.

  • Machine learning for wireless systems.
