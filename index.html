About me

I am currently working as a Lead Engineer with WiSig Networks at IIT Hyderabad, where I am involved in algorithm design and development for 4G and 5G systems. I received my Ph.D. and M.Tech degrees from Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad under the guidance of Prof. Kiran Kuchi.

Research Areas

  • Wireless communications.

  • Massive MIMO and beamforming.

  • Scheduler designs for cellular technoligies.
